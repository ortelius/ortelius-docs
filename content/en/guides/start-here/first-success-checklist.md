Use this checklist to confirm your initial setup is complete and data is flowing correctly. Each item links to the relevant setup step if you need to revisit it.

- [ ] **Account created** and you can log in — see [Log In for the First Time](../log-in-for-the-first-time/)
- [ ] **GitHub connected** — see [Connect GitHub](../connect-github/)
- [ ] **Repositories selected** and onboarded — see [Select Repositories to Watch](../select-repositories-to-watch/)
- [ ] **Releases and endpoints appear** on your dashboard — see [Verify Your Data](../verify-your-data/)
- [ ] **SBOM data attached** to at least one release — see [Attach or Discover SBOMs](../attach-or-discover-sboms/)
- [ ] **Deployment tracking configured**, if applicable — see [Track Deployments](../track-deployments/)
- [ ] **Vulnerability sync confirmed** — CVE counts appear against your components — see [Sync Vulnerability Data](../sync-vulnerability-data/)
- [ ] **Dashboard reads correctly** — you understand the executive summary cards and severity breakdown — see [Read Your Dashboard](../../using-ortelius/read-your-dashboard/)
- [ ] **Team invited** — colleagues can log in with appropriate roles — see [Invite Your Team](../../using-ortelius/invite-your-team/)

If you're seeing zero CVEs after completing the above, see the troubleshooting note in [Sync Vulnerability Data](../sync-vulnerability-data/) — SBOM data typically needs one scan cycle (about 15 minutes) after a repo is connected before CVE matching appears.
