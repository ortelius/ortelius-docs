The hosted version at **[app.deployhub.com](https://app.deployhub.com)** requires no infrastructure setup. Sign up, connect GitHub, and you have real vulnerability data within minutes.

This is the right choice if:

- You want to evaluate Ortelius quickly
- You don't need to self-manage the database, backend, or scanner components
- Your data can live in the hosted environment

## Sign up

1. Go to [app.deployhub.com](https://app.deployhub.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your username, email, first name, last name, and organization name
3. Check your email for an invitation link — click it to set your password and log in
4. You're in. Your organization is created and you are its owner.

> **Note:** If your organization already exists in the system, you'll see a conflict message with the org admin's email address. Contact them directly to be added.

Alternatively, click **Create New Account** directly on the login page. Either way, after activating your account you'll land on the **Welcome** page, which already has sample data loaded — see [Quick Tour with Sample Data](../../quick-tour-with-sample-data/).
