- **GitHub Issues** — [ortelius/ortelius](https://github.com/ortelius/ortelius/issues) is used for issues across the whole project, including the backend, frontend, and scanner components.
- **Discord** — join the community at [discord.gg/ortelius](https://discord.gg/ortelius) for questions and discussion.
- **Documentation feedback** — if a page in these docs is wrong or missing something, open an issue on [ortelius/ortelius-docs](https://github.com/ortelius/ortelius-docs/issues).
- **Security disclosures** — email security@ortelius.io.

If you're not sure whether something is a bug, a configuration issue, or expected behavior, check [Troubleshooting](../../reference/troubleshooting/) and the [FAQ](../../reference/faq/) first.

Want to contribute back rather than just ask for help? See the [Contributor Guide](../../developer-resources/contributor-guide/).
